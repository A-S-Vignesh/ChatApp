Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information AetherChat ("we", "the app") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. AetherChat is a one-to-one messaging app. By using it, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Profile information you add. An optional bio, phone number, location, and date of birth, if you choose to fill them in.
  • Messages. The content of messages you send, along with delivery and read status, so we can deliver them and show receipts.
  • Push subscription. If you enable notifications, we store the browser push subscription needed to deliver them.
  • Technical data. Basic information such as connection and presence (online/last-seen) needed to operate real-time chat.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the service: authenticate you, deliver your messages, and show profiles and presence to people you chat with.
  • To send push notifications for new messages (only when enabled and only when you are not actively viewing that chat).
  • To keep the service secure and prevent abuse (e.g. rate limiting).

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not use your messages to build advertising profiles.

4. Who can see your data

The messages you send are visible to the participants of that conversation. Your name, profile picture, and the profile fields you fill in are visible to people you chat with. Your presence (online status, last seen, read receipts, typing) is governed by your privacy settings below.

5. Your privacy controls

In Settings → Privacy you control four signals, and each one is mutual — turning it off stops you from sending that signal and stops you from seeing it from others:

  • Read receipts
  • Online status
  • Last seen
  • Typing indicator

6. Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete account. Deleting your account removes your account record, your messages, your push subscriptions, and any chat where you were the only remaining participant. This action cannot be undone.

7. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Please note that messages are not end-to-end encrypted: they are stored so they can be delivered across your devices. You should not use AetherChat to share highly sensitive information.

8. Cookies

We use a single authentication cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children

AetherChat is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are under that age.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
